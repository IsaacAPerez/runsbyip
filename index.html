About RunsByIP

RunsByIP is a weekly pickup basketball community in Los Angeles, organized by Isaac Perez. Every Wednesday, we book a gym and run 5v5 games. Payment covers your share of the gym rental — no hassle, no chasing people down. Just RSVP, pay, and show up ready to play.

Refund Policy

All refunds are $9 of your $10. The $1 difference covers non-recoverable processing fees.

  • • Can't make it? No worries — you get $9 back.
  • • Session cancelled by us? You still get $9 back.
  • • Refund requests must be made before the session starts.

To request a refund, reach out on Instagram or email hello@runsbyip.com.
